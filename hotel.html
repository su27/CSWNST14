There are FREE shuttle bus from airport/Gongbei port to the hotels.

From hotels to university campus: bus 71 (orange sign), 72(blue sign),
will stop around Cotai and in front of N1 at University of Macau.

We will provide shuttle bus between university campus and hotels.
Detailed timetable will be released according to the accommodation information.
