General Information

The 14th Cross-Strait Workshop on "Nano Science and Technology" (CSWNST11) will be held at University of Macau from 21-24 June 2017.

Cross-Strait Workshop on "Nano Science and Technology" is a yearly international conference on Nano Science and Technology, which is highly admired and held in turn among Mainland China, Hong Kong and Taiwan. This is the first time that Macau will join the big group and hold the 14th conference.

The program will include oral and poster sessions on the areas of Electronic Materials, Energy and Environment Materials, Bio-Materials, Advanced Functional Materials, Advanced Structure Materials, etc.
